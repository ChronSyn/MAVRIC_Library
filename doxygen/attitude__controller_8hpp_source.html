00032 /*******************************************************************************
00033  * \file attitude_controller.hpp
00034  *
00035  * \author MAV'RIC Team
00036  * \author Julien Lecoeur
00037  *
00038  * \brief A cascaded controller for attitude & rate control.
00039  *
00040  * \details It takes a command in attitude (roll/pitch/yaw or quaternion) as
00041  * input, and computes a torque command on roll pitch and yaw.
00042  * The inner PID loop controls the angular speed around the 3 axis. This inner
00043  * loop is fed by the outer PID loop which controls the attitude.
00044  * The error of the outer loop is computed using quaternion arithmetic, and thus
00045  * avoids gimbal locks as long as the attitude error is smaller than 90 degrees
00046  * on the pitch axis.
00047  *
00048  ******************************************************************************/
